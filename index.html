My name is Anna Sikkink, I graduated in Spring 2017 from the University of Hawaii at Mānoa with a BA in Information and Computer Science (IT focus), and a minor in Astronomy.

I began taking graduate classes in Fall 2019, and my current goal is to complete an MS project which combines my Computer Science and Astronomy knowledge.

I am currently employed at the University of Hawaii at Mānoa as an IT Specialist for Student Housing. My main focus is on web application development for internal usage.
